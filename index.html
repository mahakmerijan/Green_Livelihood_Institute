About Us

Meet the team driving growth, learning, and innovation at Green Livelihoods Institute.

Who We Are

Green Livelihoods Institute (GLI) is a centre for knowledge, attitude, skills, and impact. We work at the intersection of enterprise development, sustainable agriculture, digital innovation, and grassroots capacity building. Our multidisciplinary team brings together practitioners, policy experts, and technologists united by a common purpose — creating equitable and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions categorised by target sector — tailored to your context and goals.

🌾 Agriculture & Rural Livelihoods
Value Chain Development

End-to-end design and management of agricultural value chains from farm to market, integrating smallholders into formal markets.

FPO Capacity Building

Strengthening Farmer Producer Organisations through governance training, business planning, and market linkage support.

Sustainable Farming Practices

Promoting climate-resilient and regenerative agriculture practices for long-term soil health and productivity.

🏭 MSME & Enterprise Development
Business Diagnostics & Strategy

Deep-dive assessments of enterprise performance followed by actionable growth strategies and implementation roadmaps.

Market Access & Linkages

Connecting MSMEs with buyers, investors, and institutional markets through curated networking and deal facilitation.

Entrepreneurship Mentoring

Structured mentoring programmes for early-stage entrepreneurs covering ideation, validation, and scale-up strategies.

🤖 Digital & Technology
Data Analytics & AI Advisory

Helping organisations leverage data for decision-making, predictive analytics, and AI-enabled service delivery.

Digital Transformation

Guiding enterprises through digital adoption — from process automation to e-commerce and digital marketing strategies.

Tech-for-Good Consulting

Designing technology solutions that address social challenges in health, education, agriculture, and financial inclusion.

Leadership Development
